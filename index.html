Professional Ad Making

How We Make Ad

Pre-Production (Planning Phase) :

1. Understanding the Brand & Audience : Research the target audience, their pain points, and preferences.

2. Concept & Storyboarding : Develop a strong concept and create a visual storyboard.

3. Scriptwriting : Craft a compelling script with a clear message and call to action (CTA).

3. Casting & Location Scouting : Choose the right actors, voice-over artists, and locations for shooting.

Production (Shooting Phase) :

Filming Techniques :

1. Rule of Thirds : Use balanced composition for engaging visuals.

2. Lighting & Cinematography : Ensure proper lighting for a professional look.

3. Camera Angles & Movements : Use creative angles like close-ups, tracking shots, and drone footage to make the ad dynamic.

4. Green Screen & Special Effects : If needed, use a green screen to add CGI elements.

Audio Elements : Clear dialogues, High-quality voice-overs, Background music that suits the theme, Sound effects for impact

Post-Production (Editing & Final Touches) :

Editing Techniques :

1. Cut-to-Action : Keep it fast-paced and engaging.

2. Transitions & Motion Graphics : Smooth transitions and creative animations.

3. Color Grading : Adjust colors for a cinematic feel.

4. Text & Typography : Highlight key messages using animated text.

Final Optimization : Add logos, social media handles, and website links.
